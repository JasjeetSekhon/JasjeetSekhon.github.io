The nonaccelerating inflation rate of unemployment (NAIRU) is an important economic and political concept. It is significant not only because most macroeconomists use it when thinking about a variety of issues but also because it plays a pivotal role in guiding policy. In this note I briefly describe how I estimate the NAIRU.
